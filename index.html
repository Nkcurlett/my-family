About Emma Jean Houston


Emma Houston was born on October 10, 1928 to the union of Homer Sanders and Mattie Cooney in Biscoe Arkansas. She was educated in the Biscoe Public School System and professed a hope in Christ at an early age. One son Melvin Houston preceded her in death.


Emma touched the lives of all those who knew her. She was a loving and giving person. Everyone looked up to her and never hesitated to ask her for her help on their favorite recipe. She enjoyed spending time with her family and friend as well as traveling and watching her favorite TV shows. She trusted and believed in the LORD, never complained no matter the circumstance. Not only was she a Mother, she was also a Friend, Provider, Teacher; actually she was "One in a Million".
